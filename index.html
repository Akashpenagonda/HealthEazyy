Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our programs and approach

Can I lose 10 kgs in 3 months?

Yes, if you stay consistent with our personalized plan. Your body will respond positively with science-backed strategies designed specifically for you. We've helped hundreds of clients achieve similar results through sustainable methods.

I'm a busy working professional/student and don't have time to cook. Will this work for me?

Absolutely! We customize your plan according to your lifestyle—whether you eat mess/hostel food or have very little time to cook. You'll also get access to our special 10-minute recipe guide designed just for busy professionals like you.

Will I have to give up my favorite foods?

No! Your favorite foods are mindfully included. We believe in balance, not restriction. No boring or tasteless diets—everything you enjoy can be part of your plan. We'll show you how to enjoy your favorites while still making progress.

Will this be a strict or crash diet plan?

Never. We don't believe in extreme calorie cuts or crash diets. Our approach is gentle, science-backed, and designed for long-term, sustainable results—not short-term suffering. You'll eat satisfying meals and still see amazing results.

Will I have to buy supplements, shakes, or fat burners?

No! We strictly do NOT promote or sell any products, shakes, or fat burners. Your results will come from real food, natural methods, and lifestyle changes backed by science not market gimmicks. We're proud to be product-free.

Will this plan help with PCOS, thyroid, hair fall, or skin issues?

Yes. Each plan is customized to heal root causes like hormonal imbalance, stress, and nutrient deficiencies helping manage PCOS, thyroid, hair thinning, acne, and other concerns naturally. Many clients see significant improvements in these areas.

Is workout necessary? I'm a beginner/very busy.

Yes—but don't worry! Workouts are beginner-friendly, flexible, and can be done at home or in the gym suited to your energy, time, and comfort level. We'll create a movement plan that fits your lifestyle perfectly.

Will this plan fit my busy lifestyle/hostel/work life?

Absolutely. The plan is designed to fit you whether you're living in a hostel, working full-time, or managing family life. No fancy equipment or unrealistic recipes. We specialize in making health work with real life constraints.

Will I get personal guidance, or is this a generic package?

You get personal, 1-on-1 guidance from our expert coaches. No copy-paste plans, no automation, no being left alone after payment. Your progress is monitored, updated, and supported fully throughout your journey with us.

How is HealthEazyy different from other online diet programs?

We focus on root cause healing, not symptoms. No crash diets, no false promises, no pushing products—only real, sustainable transformation customized to your unique body and needs. Our clients achieve lasting results, not temporary fixes.
